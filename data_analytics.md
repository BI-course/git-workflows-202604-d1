
# Basics of Data Analytics 

**Data analytics** is the process of collecting, cleaning, and analyzing data to support better business decisions. In business intelligence (BI), it focuses on turning raw data into meaningful insights.

### 1. Types of Data Analytics

* **Descriptive** – explains what has happened (e.g., sales reports)
* **Diagnostic** – explains why it happened
* **Predictive** – forecasts future outcomes
* **Prescriptive** – suggests actions to take


### 2. Key Tools & Technologies

* **Microsoft Excel** – data organization, quick analysis
* **SQL** – extracting data from databases
* **Python** / **R** – deeper analysis
* **Power BI** / **Tableau** – dashboards and visualization


### 3. Core Concepts

* Data cleaning (fix errors, missing values)
* Data transformation (organizing data)
* Basic statistics (averages, trends, relationships)
* Data visualization (charts, dashboards)


### 4. Analytics Process

1. Define the business problem
2. Collect relevant data
3. Clean and prepare data
4. Analyze and identify patterns
5. Visualize and communicate insights


### 5. Role in Business Intelligence

Data analytics helps organizations:

* Monitor performance (KPIs)
* Identify trends and opportunities
* Support decision-making
* Improve efficiency and strategy
